About this Site

This website is the final product of the "Personal Website" project for CIS 191, Web Development: Client Side at Messiah University. This project went through a multiple-step process, starting with a rough draft, then a critique, then a final draft, and finally the HTML and CSS design. It was a lot of fun to plan out every step to make the development process as smooth as possible. I hope you enjoy the site!
