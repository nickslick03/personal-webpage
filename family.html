Nicholas' family has made him who he is. Well, his family and StarFox 64. Here's the lineup:

Michael Epps
Nicholas' charasmatic yet down-to-earth Father. He loves talking to all kinds of people and hearing their stories and his ultimate goal in life is to be offered a cup of coffee from a stranger, chating with them on their front porch. He and Nicholas enjoy camping, mountain biking, and entering grades in Genesis together.
Larisa Epps
Detail-oriented, family-oriented, and musically-oriented; Nicholas has the best mother in the world. She has reared him and his siblings through countless chores, many a school assignments, years of violin lessons and so much more. All without drinking coffee. She and Nicholas enjoy playing the violin together and playing Bananagrams (unless she wins, which she usually does).
Nathanael Epps
Nicholas' older brother is the laziest (or most efficient) programmer. He is currently writing his own C++ Compiler that will put GNU to shame. These two keep it real with a game of Call of Duty Black Ops Zombies, Grilling kabobs, or crusing downtown with the top down.
Catherine Epps
Catherine is the artist of the family, having made name for herself in the competitive illustration scene of Philadelphia with her debut comicbook lil and friend die for real. She makes a mean bacon mac 'n' cheese and plays a mean yoshi in Mario Kart 8.
Christian Epps
The youngest of the family, this 6th grader bangs on his keyboard after losing in Roblox almost as hard as he bangs on the drums. He also enjoys a good Redwall book next to a fire, with a nice glass of jugo de naranja.
